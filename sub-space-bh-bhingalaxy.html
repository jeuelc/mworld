The Black Hole in Our Galaxy

 

Yes, there is a black hole at the centre of our galaxy a very, very large black hole! But dont worry, its very very far away. In fact its so far away it will never come close enough to be a threat to the Earth.

There are also other black holes in our galaxy, but these are much smaller than the black hole at the centre of our galaxy. These black holes are also very far away. You see, the Universe really is a very large place.

What would happen if a black hole hit the Earth? If it was a big black hole, it would swallow the Earth. Luckily, this is very unlikely to ever happen.

If a small black hole hit the Earth it would pass straight through the Earth, and keep on going. The Universe really is a strange place too!
