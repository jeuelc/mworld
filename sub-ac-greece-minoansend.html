The end of The Minoans

No one really knows for sure how the Minoan civilisation ended. Most historians think it was linked to the huge explosion of a volcano on the island of Thera.

Today Thera is called Santorini. It's a beautiful island, not far from the mainland of Greece. But about 3,600 years ago this island was part of a large volcano that exploded and sent ash and a large tidal wave across nearby islands.

The damage from the explosion weakened the Minoan civilisation, allowing the Mycenaeans to become culturally dominant.
